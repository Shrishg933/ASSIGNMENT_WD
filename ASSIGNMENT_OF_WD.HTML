Travel Terms
Visa
A visa is an official authorization that allows entry into, or stay within, a country for specific purposes such as tourism, business, study, or medical reasons.
Itinerary
An itinerary is a detailed plan or schedule for a journey, outlining the route, places to visit, transportation arrangements, accommodations, and activities for each day.
Jet Lag
Jet lag is a temporary sleep disorder that occurs when the body's internal clock is out of sync with a new time zone after rapidly traveling across several time zones by air.
